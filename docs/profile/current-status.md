**Obligation:** Recommended

**Definition:**<br>
Information concerning the current status of the sample. Does the sample still exist? Was it discarded/destroyed in analysis or otherwise? Has it degraded over time or been lost?

Note that it is recommended to also indicate the date that the value of the status was revised.

**Allowed values, examples, other constraints:**<br>
Archived, Destroyed,...

# 21.2 accessConditions

**Obligation:** Recommended

**Definition:**<br>
What the access rights are to the sample and/or who to contact about access. Access rights should ideally follow a controlled vocabulary.

**Allowed values, examples, other constraints:**<br>
Open access, Embargoed,...

Access to the sample is upon request. Please contact the Department of History and Archaeology, Macquarie University.

## 21.2.1 accessType

**Obligation:** Optional

**Definition:**<br>
Whether the sample is publicly available or only for private use. A binary choice to enable a quick understanding of access rights.

**Allowed values, examples, other constraints:**<br>
Public / Private
